To play the webgl game 
first install
a small local server starter 

https://greggman.github.io/servez/

after installing select the folder with webgl build
and then lauch in browser


the controls are 

left/right click to rotate and move chairs and laptops
"q" to pickup/drop main laptop
"e" to pickup and drop cups
right click to unlock door


objective
exit the room

hints
place cups in place
place chairs in place
rotate the laptops

look at the map on the mainlaptop and on the wall to figure out how the room is supposed to be
